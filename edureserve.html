Edu Reserve - Student Study Room Booking System

**Edu Reserve** is a smart booking platform designed to provide students at **NUI (National University Institute)** with an easy and efficient way to reserve study rooms. The system ensures that there are no conflicts in room bookings, allowing students to focus on their studies without the hassle of double bookings or confusion. Additionally, the system allows students to leave their feedback, improving the quality and functionality of the platform.

Key Features of Edu Reserve:

  • Room Booking: Students can easily book study rooms available within the campus for specific times and dates. The system ensures that there are no overlaps in booking, so each student has their own private space to study.
  • Conflict-Free Scheduling: The system features an automatic conflict detection mechanism, which checks the availability of rooms and prevents double-booking.
  • Real-Time Availability: Room availability is updated in real-time, ensuring that students can see which rooms are free and which are already booked at any given time.
  • Booking Duration: Students can choose the duration for their booking, and the system ensures that no student can reserve a room for excessive periods, allowing fair access for all.
  • Student Feedback: After their study session, students can leave feedback about the room, its environment, and the booking process. This feedback is used to improve the system and provide better service.
  • Notifications: Students receive email or SMS notifications confirming their bookings and reminders before their scheduled time to help them stay on track.
  • Easy-to-Use Interface: The platform provides a user-friendly interface that allows students to view available rooms, make bookings, and provide feedback with ease.

Benefits of Edu Reserve:

  • Improved Study Environment: Students can secure a quiet, dedicated space for focused study sessions, enhancing productivity.
  • Efficient Use of Resources: The system ensures that rooms are used optimally, with no wasted time due to double bookings or unused slots.
  • Convenient Scheduling: Students can plan their study time in advance, ensuring they can find a suitable room at their preferred time without conflicts.
  • Feedback-Driven Improvement: The feedback system enables continuous improvement of the platform and the study rooms, ensuring that student needs are met.
  • Fair Access for All Students: By limiting the booking duration and offering real-time updates, Edu Reserve ensures that all students have equal opportunities to reserve a study room.

**Edu Reserve** empowers students at **NUI** to make the most of their study time, offering them the flexibility and convenience of booking study rooms with no conflicts. With its focus on user experience, real-time availability, and feedback integration, Edu Reserve plays a crucial role in enhancing the learning environment on campus.
