Edu Reserve - Student Study Room Booking System

Edu Reserve is a smart booking platform designed to provide students at UCSI university with an easy and efficient way to reserve study rooms. The system ensures that there are no conflicts in room bookings, allowing students to focus on their studies without the hassle of double bookings or confusion. Additionally, the system allows students to leave their feedback, improving the quality and functionality of the platform.

Key Features of Edu Reserve:

  • Room Booking
  • Conflict-Free Scheduling
  • Real-Time Availability
  • Booking Duration
  • Student Feedback
  • Notifications
  • Easy-to-Use Interface

Benefits of Edu Reserve:

  • Students can secure a quiet, dedicated space for focused study sessions, enhancing productivity.
  • The system ensures that rooms are used optimally, with no wasted time due to double bookings or unused slots.
  • Students can plan their study time in advance, ensuring they can find a suitable room at their preferred time without conflicts.
  • The feedback system enables continuous improvement of the platform and the study rooms, ensuring that student needs are met.
  • By limiting the booking duration and offering real-time updates, Edu Reserve ensures that all students have equal opportunities to reserve a study room.
